Comparison of Synphot and Pysynphot Bandpar Functionality¶

Author: Matt Davis (SSB)

Date: 15 November 2011

Abstract

Pysynphot attempts to replicate much of the functionality of the Synphot bandpar utility but sometimes uses different formulae and algorithms. This TSR collects the calculations used in Pysynphot, Synphot, and the formula described in the Synphot Manual on page 42.
